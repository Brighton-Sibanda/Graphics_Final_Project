Twilight Departure: From a cozy cottage into the Cosmos

My model contains a cottage, stars, fireflies, and a spaceship.

Usage Instructions:

  • Camera Controls: Use WASD to move forward/backward/left/right, R to go up, and F to go down
  • Light Controls: Use the sliders below to adjust the light position and properties
  • White Light: Toggle the checkbox to enable/disable the white light source
  • Spaceship: Click the Launch button to send the spaceship into space!
